# Lotus Private Relocation — Unit Economics v0.1

## Purpose

This model separates cash collected for Lotus services from contingent referral income. It is a planning model, not a promise of market demand or partner compensation.

## Revenue categories

| Category | Recognition rule |
|---|---|
| Discovery fee | Earned after the session and written brief are delivered |
| Decision Map fee | Earned against the agreed delivery milestones |
| Coordination fee | Earned against the engagement scope and coordination period |
| B2B desk fee | Earned under the adviser agreement and completed case allowance |
| Referral commission | Earned only after the written partner trigger is satisfied and evidenced |
| Paid commission | Cash actually received and reconciled |

`forecast`, `earned`, and `paid` must remain separate states.

## Pilot assumptions

These figures are hypotheses for a controlled launch:

- Relocation Discovery: EUR 250;
- UAE Decision Map: EUR 650;
- Family Relocation Coordination: EUR 1,500 starting price;
- B2B Decision Desk: EUR 750 monthly pilot retainer;
- direct delivery cost: primarily founder time;
- payment-processing, tax, accounting, and professional-review costs are not yet included;
- referral commission is excluded from the base operating plan.

## Referral upside model

Referral income is calculated only from the licensed partner's documented commission and the signed introducer formula.

Example only:

```text
partner commission actually received: AED 50,000
signed Lotus share: 25%
referral amount before tax and fees: AED 12,500
```

A public website statement does not establish the signed share, calculation base, trigger, payout date, tax treatment, or clawback terms.

## Founder-capacity guardrail

Before increasing sales volume, record:

- hours spent per service;
- research and call time;
- revision requests;
- partner-coordination time;
- payment and administration time;
- professional-review costs;
- refund or dispute incidence.

A package is commercially viable only when the client outcome is repeatable and delivery does not require unbounded founder time.

## Stop conditions

Pause sales and review the offer if:

- clients believe the fee purchases brokerage, visa, legal, tax, or investment advice;
- delivery requires sensitive documents that Lotus is not authorised to hold;
- a partner requests unconsented client data;
- fee or referral compensation cannot be disclosed consistently;
- a paid deliverable cannot be completed without unsupported claims;
- refund or complaint patterns indicate the offer is misleading.
