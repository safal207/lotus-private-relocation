## Common activation gate

A candidate may move to `PILOT` only when all of the following are evidenced:

1. exact legal entity and current authority are independently verified;
2. written agreement is approved or signed;
3. lead registration and protection are explicit;
4. commission basis, trigger, evidence and payout timing are explicit;
5. privacy roles, consent language and Türkiye-to-UAE transfer terms are resolved;
6. the licensed partner owns KYC, AML, sanctions and source-of-funds decisions;
7. synthetic registration, acknowledgement, withdrawal and escalation tests pass;
8. no blocking risk flag remains.

Until then:

```text
no real client data
→ no real handoff
→ no commission forecast
→ no property recommendation represented as Lotus brokerage advice
```
