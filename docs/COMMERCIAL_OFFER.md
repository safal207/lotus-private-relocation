# Lotus Private Relocation — Commercial Offer v0.1

## Positioning

Lotus Private Relocation provides evidence-led decision preparation and coordination for founders, investors, and families considering a move or property purchase in the UAE.

Lotus does **not** provide regulated property brokerage, legal, tax, immigration, banking, investment, KYC, AML, sanctions, source-of-funds, or transaction-execution services. Those services remain with appropriately licensed or qualified partners.

## Revenue model

The commercial model combines:

1. paid decision-preparation services;
2. paid coordination services;
3. disclosed referral compensation from licensed partners, where a written agreement permits it;
4. white-label support for professional advisers and relocation intermediaries.

Paid client work must stand on its own value. Referral income is contingent, delayed, and never treated as earned until the contractual trigger is evidenced.

## Package 1 — Relocation Discovery

**Pilot price:** EUR 250

### Client outcome

A structured 60-minute session followed by a concise written decision brief.

### Included

- objective, destination, timing, and budget-band clarification;
- family, school, work, lifestyle, and property-use priorities;
- material unknowns and decision blockers;
- adviser and licensed-partner dependencies;
- recommended next action;
- written brief delivered within two business days.

### Excluded

- property recommendations or negotiations;
- legal, tax, immigration, financing, or investment advice;
- visa or bank-account promises;
- document review requiring a licensed professional;
- collection of passports, bank statements, or source-of-funds files.

### Payment trigger

100% paid before the session. The fee is earned by delivery of the session and written brief, independent of any later property transaction.

## Package 2 — UAE Decision Map

**Pilot price:** EUR 650

### Client outcome

A documented comparison of realistic relocation paths and the professionals required to execute them.

### Included

- everything in Relocation Discovery;
- comparison of up to three UAE location or relocation scenarios;
- neighbourhood and family-infrastructure research using public evidence;
- broad property-use and budget-fit analysis without brokerage advice;
- risk and dependency register;
- shortlist of partner categories;
- one 45-minute review session;
- seven calendar days of clarification support.

### Excluded

- guarantee of property availability, residency, financing, tax outcome, return, or school admission;
- regulated recommendations;
- handling client funds;
- contacting a named partner before separate introduction consent.

### Payment trigger

50% before work starts and 50% before the final review session.

## Package 3 — Family Relocation Coordination

**Pilot price:** from EUR 1,500

### Client outcome

A managed preparation and introduction process with named owners, milestones, and evidence.

### Included

- everything in UAE Decision Map;
- coordination of agreed licensed or qualified providers;
- preparation of minimum-data introduction briefs;
- explicit consent records for each introduction;
- milestone and next-action tracking;
- school, neighbourhood, moving, and settling-in research;
- up to four coordination calls over 30 days;
- handoff and acknowledgement evidence.

### Excluded

- acting as broker, lawyer, tax adviser, immigration consultant, banker, investment adviser, or compliance decision-maker;
- signing on behalf of the client;
- holding deposits or purchase funds;
- guaranteeing third-party delivery.

### Payment trigger

50% at engagement and 50% after delivery of the agreed coordination pack or completion of the 30-day coordination window, whichever occurs first.

## B2B — White-Label UAE Decision Desk

**Pilot models:**

- EUR 750 monthly retainer for up to three qualified cases;
- EUR 300 per completed decision brief;
- custom case fee for complex family or executive relocation;
- reciprocal or disclosed referral compensation under a written agreement.

### Intended partners

- immigration and cross-border law firms;
- tax and accounting advisers;
- fractional CFOs and corporate-service providers;
- founder communities;
- international schools and education advisers;
- relocation and global-mobility providers;
- business brokers and family-office advisers.

### B2B value

The professional adviser keeps the primary client relationship while Lotus prepares a structured UAE decision brief, coordinates consented introductions, and records ownership of the next action.

## Commercial safeguards

- written scope and fee before work begins;
- no hidden compensation;
- partner referral compensation disclosed where required;
- no fee tied to evading tax, sanctions, AML, reporting, or capital controls;
- no commission forecast without a signed agreement and defined calculation base;
- no real-client introduction to a partner below `PILOT` status;
- client may withdraw introduction consent before data transfer;
- all sensitive documents remain outside the public repository.

## Pilot success criteria

The offer is validated when Lotus completes:

- three paid Discovery engagements;
- at least two delivered decision briefs;
- one B2B adviser pilot;
- one signed licensed-partner referral agreement;
- zero unauthorised data transfers or regulated claims.
