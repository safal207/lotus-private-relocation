# Lotus Private Relocation

Evidence-led property introduction and relocation coordination for global founders, investors, and families.

## Mission

Lotus Private Relocation helps internationally mobile entrepreneurs, investors, and families navigate property-led relocation through trusted, licensed local partners.

The project is designed as a consent-based introduction and coordination layer — not as an unlicensed brokerage, legal practice, tax advisory, immigration consultancy, bank, or investment adviser.

## Initial corridor

**Global founders and families → UAE property and relocation → licensed local partners**

The first market focus is Dubai and the wider UAE. Additional jurisdictions may be added only after partner, legal, operational, and evidence checks are complete.

## What the service coordinates

- client needs and relocation-goal discovery;
- jurisdiction and location comparison;
- property search requirements;
- introductions to licensed real-estate professionals;
- introductions to qualified legal, tax, immigration, banking, education, and relocation specialists;
- progress tracking, evidence, consent, and partner accountability.

## Operating boundaries

Lotus Private Relocation does **not**:

- represent itself as a licensed real-estate broker where it is not licensed;
- provide legal, tax, immigration, banking, or investment advice;
- accept or move client funds;
- promise visas, returns, approvals, residency, or transaction outcomes;
- publish or promote property inventory without the required licensed partner and approvals;
- buy, sell, reserve, or sign on behalf of a client;
- bypass KYC, AML, sanctions, source-of-funds, or local regulatory controls;
- collect or transfer personal data without a clear purpose and consent.

## Core principle

> Evidence before recommendation. Consent before introduction. Licensed execution before transaction.

## Status

Early-stage public product and operating-system repository. No public property inventory and no client personal data are stored here.
